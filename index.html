About Me

Hi guys, my name is Ohwi Daniel (I'll prefer Danny Jay), I'm an Artist with over 3 years of experience. My experience spans across painting and pencil work, such as; Oil painting, Fabric painting and so on.

Alongside that, I also love meeting new people and going for adventures. I learnt from great Artists around, i.e My boss (Vision Art Effect). Apart from him, I also have some other friends or I'll just say some other Artists like me who are also good, they are as follows: BeeJay Art and Surprise (BAS), HezekiahArt, et cetera.

Now, moving on to my personality. My personality is a simple and easygoing one. I possess a good sense of humor, I love to socialize with new people, I also love chatting, I'm a fun person to chat with. The more I get to know someone, the more comfortable I'll feel around them.
